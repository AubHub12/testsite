Multicellular Organism

I am but a spec of dust in this vast Universe.

I seek to find a place where man is driven by his natural instincts and not by the unnecessarily complicated intellect of the human mind. Though many might argue that as humans, life should be a quest to seek answers, to find out who we are , and to find purpose. But I disagree. It is pointless. All this is nothing but a mirage, an illusion. The best way to remain sane is to let go of the attachments and live life simple, just like the other species of the planet.
